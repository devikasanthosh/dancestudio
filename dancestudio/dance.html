ABOUT

Dance Studio began in a basement studio in 2020. Since then, it has continued to
grow and welcome students of all ages, backgrounds and experience levels. Located
in Eramalloor, Kerala,India, Dance studio facility holds two fully equipped studios
and a full-time staff who speaks both English,Malayalam and Hindi. Dance Studio invites
you to explore your creative potential and find a community among mentors and peers that
never cease to INSPIRE MILLIONS.





TYPE OF CLASSES





LEARNER

Learner classes are taught at a beginner/intermediate level and are best for those who can follow along to the basics of dance. This class is great for those who have some experience but aren't quite confident in pursuing a fast-paced class just yet. By learning choreography that focuses on the basics, you will strengthen your foundational skills and improve your ability to pick up and retain choreography.
